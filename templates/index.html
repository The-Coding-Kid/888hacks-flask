SmartChoice

A website to help make smarter eating choices

Teaches better eating habits

Decades of studies have shown the need for nutritious meals amongst high schoolers, and poor nutrition leads to reduced concentration and poorer grades. With Fresh choices anyone can make better food choices.

Easy to use

This website is really easy to use. Just upload an image of your food, and wait for our backend to show you the results on how nutritious your food is. We give your food a star rating out of five, where 1 means it is not really nutritious, and 5 means it is very nutritious.

Built with state of the art technology

The website is built with Flask, a new and upcoming web framework adopted by tech giants such as Reddit. An image that you upload will be processed by our backend, and we give a star rating based on how nutritious your food is.
